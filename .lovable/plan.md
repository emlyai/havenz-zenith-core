

# Havenz Corp — Website Redesign

A refined, light, and professional corporate website with bold immersive animations, reflecting Havenz's positioning in sovereign AI, infrastructure, compute, and sustainable smart communities.

---

## Design System
- **Style**: Light & clean with generous whitespace, elegant typography, and a sophisticated color palette (deep navy/charcoal accents on white)
- **Animations**: Bold scroll-triggered reveals, parallax hero sections, animated counters for milestones, smooth section transitions, and hover effects on cards/CTAs
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif with large, impactful headings and refined body text

---

## Pages

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero Section**: Full-viewport hero with a bold headline about pioneering sustainable development and sovereign AI. Animated text reveal and a prominent "Let's Talk" CTA
- **Value Propositions**: Animated cards highlighting Food Security, Energy Sustainability, Data Integrity, and Job Stability
- **Key Stats / Milestones**: Animated counters (acres managed, year founded, projects in pipeline)
- **Partnership Highlight**: Feature the Energy Haven partnership with a visually striking split section
- **Partners Logo Strip**: Scrolling or staggered-reveal logos (GGA, Telus, Samsung, Century 21, etc.)
- **CTA Banner**: Bold call-to-action section driving visitors to Contact

### 2. About Us Page
- **Company Story**: Elegant intro about Havenz's mission and ESG+R approach
- **Interactive Timeline**: Animated vertical timeline (2010 → 2026) with milestone cards that reveal on scroll
- **Integrated Development Approach**: Section explaining the integrator model with parallax imagery
- **Team Section**: Clean grid of team members with hover reveal for roles
- **Partners & Collaborators**: Logo grid with subtle animations

### 3. Services Page
- **Overview**: Introduction to Havenz's service model across asset classes
- **Service Cards**: Animated cards for key offerings — Smart Communities, Green Energy, ESG+R Compliance, Data-Driven Development
- **Scope 1, 2, 3 Alignment**: Visual section showing regulatory alignment and environmental commitment
- **Partnership Model**: How Havenz collaborates with landowners and municipalities

### 4. Contact Page
- **Contact Form**: Elegant UI-only form (Name, Email, Subject, Message) with smooth focus animations
- **Office Locations**: Three office cards with addresses (Calgary corporate, Calgary AHI HQ, Red Deer campus)
- **Media Inquiries**: Separate section for press contacts
- **Map or visual element**: Decorative location indicator

---

## Shared Components
- **Navigation**: Sticky top nav with smooth scroll, transparent-to-white on scroll, with a highlighted "Let's Talk" CTA button
- **Footer**: Corporate footer with nav links, contact info, and social links
- **Scroll Animations**: Intersection Observer-based fade-in, slide-up, and scale reveals throughout all pages
- **Mobile Responsive**: Fully responsive with a hamburger menu on mobile
